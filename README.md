## This is a multiple background colors and two pair colors inside cell of chessboard application

### `Feature`
+ White-Black and Yellow-Green colors cells of the chessboard, Just reload the application.
+ How often click a cell on the chessboard and then see the count inside the cell.
+ Change of color in cell by Mouseover
+ Change of color in cell by one click
